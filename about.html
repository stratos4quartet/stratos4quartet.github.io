Meet the Quartet

We're a group of four members of the Sound of the Rockies chorus who've expanded our harmonic escapades to the smaller, riserless stage of the quartet format. We love performing in all kinds of places, from restaurants to senior homes to offices.
  • Mike Skaff - Lead
  • Mark Palmer - Tenor
  • Kevin Kaiser - Bass
  • Paul Ashour - Baritone
